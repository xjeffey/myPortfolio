ReceiptX

After making a purchase, some people lose their receipts, making it hard for them to manage their finances. Today, most people use their smart phone in their everyday life. The problem we are trying to solve is to find a way for people between the ages 19-40 to manage their receipts and finances by creating an android application called "ReceiptX." Users will be able to take images and manually enter receipt information to the app. Just like how people check their banking information on their phone, they will be able to do the same with the receipts they have collected on the app. We found that most people do not keep of their receipts because they lose it. With ReceiptX, we hope to turn things around and make receipt management easier for people.

African Rhino Hammer

The weapon is an ancient "Rhino-Hammer" from W'Kabi's tribe that has been modified to allow the head to release and extend the length of the weapon. The weapon can also gather and charge kinetic enrgy by swinging it around and then transferring the stored potential energy is three ways: "Rhino Smash" - direct hit release, "Rhino Blash" - concentrated laser-shot release of energy through the horn of the hammer, and "Rhino Quake" - slamming the rhino horn into the ground releases energy into the surrounding environment.
