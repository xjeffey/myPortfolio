School of Informatics, Computing and Engineering
Indiana University

The School of Informatics and Computing includes the School of Informatics, Computing, and Engineering, Bloomington and the School of Informatics, IUPUI. The mission of the School is to excel and lead in education, research, and outreach spanning and integrating the full breadth of computing and information technology, including the scientific and technical core, a broad range of applications, and human and societal issues and implications.
