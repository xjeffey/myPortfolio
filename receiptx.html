Expensify

Pros

  • Submit receipts to manager
  • Collect receipts from clients
  • Track personal expenses
  • Categorization
  • Mileage and time tracking
  • Direct deposit and reimbursement

Cons

  • Scanning receipts not always accurate
  • Functionality is not always clear
  • Sometimes loses receipts

Shoeboxed

Pros

  • Check for deductible taxes
  • Mark expenses as reimbursable
  • Imports receipts from Gmail
  • Creates reports
  • Mileage tracking

Cons

  • Only PDF version when exporting data
  • Receipts are sometimes mislabeled
  • Pricey for those who do not use app often

Neat

Pros

  • Share data between people
  • Folders
  • Comments on receipts
  • Imports data from PDF files, images, and electionic receipts
  • Expense reports

Cons

  • Prices may vary
  • Must log in after 5 minute timeout
  • Quality of image determines how app functions
